
***********  Assignment  ***********

You have 20 minutes to create a short dystopian science fiction story.
To do so, write a complete and detailed prompt that includes the following elements:
- Task: Clearly define what you want the language model to do.
- Context: Provide details that establish the setting and situation of the story.
- Examples: Offer examples of what the result should look like.
- Role: Indicate the perspective from which the story should be written.
- Format: Specify the structure and expected outcome.
- Tone: Define the style or tone of the story.
- Audience: Mention who the story is intended for.
Use a language model of your choice to generate the story.
